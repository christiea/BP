Big Picture

How the project works

How

Big Picture connects an online database of people wishing to visit various locations. Although not everyone visits at the exact same time time and day, which means they may not necessarily cross physical paths but our app and website provide the opportunity to cross virtual paths and connect poeple through their shared experiences.

Big Picture is accessable as an app or a website. Within this is a vast list of various scenic/ outdoor locations. Some may be very close to you and some much further away but all are on Big Picture. Now when you first click on the first locations you will only see a few pictures and a short bio and a few tips plus the address. so you can find your way there. However, you will notice that there is a lock on the page. On the app you can click on this lock which takes you to a camera. This is where the actual act of visiting the location comes into "play".

Go to one of the various locations. On a noteable sign you will see one of our QR codes underneath our logo. After opening the app on your device or by simply opening your own camera the QR code will ask if you'd like to proceed using safari, googl or "Big Picture" choose your preference and the QR code will take you to the locations page however now that you have scanned you are now in the location 'group' and have full access to other users photos, upload your own posts and comment, like etc... Rather than a fact file you will see more of a forum where you can interact with other people.

Access

Big Picture is available on IOS, windows, and most devices with an app store.
